Tiramisu

Description

A classic Italian dessert that blends cocoa, coffee, and mascarpone into something refreshing and balanced. It's sweet, but not too sweet!

Ingredients

  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup of white sugar
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1 and 1/4 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/2 teaspoon of vanilla extract
  • 1 pound of mascarpone (room temperature)
  • 1/4 cup of strong brewed coffee (room temperature)
  • 2 tablespoons of rum
  • 2 3-ounce packages of ladyfinger cookies
  • 1 tablespoon of unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Whisk the egg yolks and sugar together in a medium saucepan until well-blended. Whisk in the milk and cook it all over medium heat (while still stirring) until the mixture comes to a boil.
  2. Boil it gently for 1 minute before removing it from the heat and letting it cool a bit before tightly covering it and setting it to chill in the refrigerator for 1 hour.
  3. Beat the cream and vanilla in a medium bowl with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form.
  4. Remove the egg yolk mixture from the refrigerator. Add in the mascarpone and whisk until smooth.
  5. Combine the coffee and rum in a small bowl. Split the ladyfingers in half (lengthwise) and drizzle them with the coffee mixture. Arrange half of them in the bottom of a 7x11-inch dish.
  6. Spread half of the mascarpone mixture over the ladyfingers, then spread half of the whipped cream on top. Repeat those layers one more time.
  7. Sprinkle the cocoa powder on top, then cover the dish and refrigerate it for about 4 to 6 hours.
  8. It's ready! Enjoy!
