Chicken Parmesan

Description

A delicious, filling dish that's perfect for cold days. If you're looking for something warm and comforting with a good amount of protein, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless and boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • A cup of panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup of grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of mozzarella cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup of grated provolone
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
