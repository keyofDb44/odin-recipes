Chicken Parmesan

Description

A delicious, filling take on chicken Parmesan that's perfect for cold days. If you're looking for something warm and comforting with a good amount of protein, this recipe is for you!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless and boneless chicken breast halves
  • 2 large eggs
  • A cup of panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 cup of grated Parmesan
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of mozzarella cut into cubes
  • 1/4 cup of chopped basil
  • 1/2 cup of grated provolone
  • 2 teaspoons of olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 450°F.
  2. Cover the chicken breasts with plastic and use a mallet to thin them down to about half an inch.
  3. Use salt and pepper to season the chicken, then coat both sides in flour.
  4. Beat the two eggs and set those aside in their bowl. Mix the panko and Parmesan in a separate bowl, then set that aside. Now dip each chicken breast first in the eggs, then in the panko mixture, before letting them rest for 15 minutes.
  5. Heat up some olive oil in a skillet on medium-high heat, then cook the chicken breasts in it for roughly two minutes on each side.
  6. Move the chicken over to a baking tray or dish before topping them each with 2 tablespoons of tomato sauce and the mozzarella, basil, and provolone. Drizzle each with 1/2 teaspoon of olive oil as well.
  7. Bake the chicken in the oven for 15 to 20 minutes or until they hit an internal temperature of 165°F. Let the chicken rest briefly before serving.
